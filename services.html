Our services

Reliable solar for island living

From the first energy audit to lifetime support, we handle every step of going solar — engineered for the salt air, sun, and storms of the islands.

End to end

From audit to activation

Eight services that cover the whole journey to energy independence — no guesswork, no gaps, all backed by a local island-based team.

01 / INSTALL

Solar Panel Installation

Harness the abundant tropical sun to power your home or business. We design and install high-efficiency photovoltaic systems specifically engineered to withstand Siargao's coastal environment, helping you eliminate high electricity bills.

02 / ROOFING

Roofing & Infrastructure

A solar system is only as strong as what it sits on. We evaluate and reinforce your roof — whether it's traditional nipa or modern concrete — to ensure it can safely support your panels and resist high island winds.

03 / STORAGE

Battery Storage

Say goodbye to island brownouts. Our premium battery backup systems store excess daytime energy so your lights, fans, and refrigerators keep running through the night or during unexpected power outages.

04 / AUDIT

Energy Audits

Stop wasting power and start saving money. Our team conducts a full analysis of your current energy consumption to identify 'vampire' loads and efficiency gaps, ensuring your new solar system is perfectly sized for your actual needs.

05 / MONITOR

Maintenance & Monitoring

Tropical salt air and dust can lower panel efficiency over time. We provide scheduled cleaning, hardware check-ups, and 24/7 digital monitoring to ensure your system is always performing at its peak.

06 / CONSULT

Consultation

Every property in Siargao is unique. We sit down with you to discuss your energy goals, budget, and site layout, providing a customized roadmap to energy independence with no guesswork involved.

07 / ACTIVATE

Activation

The most exciting step: flipping the switch. Once installation is complete, we perform rigorous safety testing and system calibration before walking you through your new setup to ensure everything is running perfectly.

08 / WARRANTY

Warranty & Support

We are here for the long haul. All our installations come with industry-leading warranties on panels and inverters, backed by a local Siargao-based team ready to provide fast, on-site support whenever you need it.
